SACRED HEART BOARDING SCHOOL

About Us

Sacred Heart aims to provide world-class quality education to the children of Nepalese families in order to produce patriotic, dexterous, versatile, disciplined and dedicated human resources that can lead the country in various walks of life. In accordance with the Educational Policy of the Government of Nepal, We has the aim of fulfilling the following objectives:

  • To impart quality education to the sons and the daughters of Nepalese Civlian
  • To produce manpower to provide able leadership in the various institutions nationally and internationally
  • To produce loyal, capable and trustworthy manpower who can shoulder the responsibilities of developing the country
  • To promote good conduct, high sense of morality, feelings of nationalism and discipline

Message from our Principal
